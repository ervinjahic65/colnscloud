About Automate Security


At Automate Security, we are driven by a singular mission: to empower organizations worldwide with unparalleled cloud security. In today's dynamic digital landscape, where multi-cloud environments are the norm, safeguarding your critical assets from ever-evolving threats is not just a necessity—it's the cornerstone of sustained innovation and business continuity. Our journey began with a profound understanding of the complexities businesses face in securing their cloud infrastructures. We saw a need for a solution that was not only robust but also intuitive, a platform that could adapt to the rapid pace of cloud adoption and the ever-changing threat landscape.


Founded on the principles of proactive protection, intelligent prioritization, and seamless automation, Automate Security emerged from a deep understanding of the challenges faced by modern enterprises. We recognized the urgent need for a unified solution that could not only identify and assess risks across diverse cloud infrastructures but also provide actionable insights for rapid remediation. Our vision was to simplify complex security paradigms, making robust cloud defense accessible and manageable for teams of all sizes, from small startups to large enterprises. We believe that true security comes from foresight and the ability to act swiftly.


Our core philosophy revolves around empowering security and DevSecOps teams. We believe that by providing comprehensive visibility into every cloud resource, from development to runtime, we enable these critical teams to operate with confidence. This includes offering agentless asset discovery that spans AWS, Azure, GCP, and Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI), ensuring no corner of your cloud environment remains unseen. Our powerful visualization tools transform raw data into a clear, unified view, allowing your team to quickly grasp even the most intricate issues and make informed decisions, reducing the time from detection to resolution.


Compliance and audit readiness are central to our platform. We understand the burden of regulatory requirements, which is why Automate Security offers automated reporting against over 20 compliance frameworks, including SOC-2, GDPR, PCI DSS, HIPAA, and NYDFS. This capability eliminates audit fire drills, allowing you to verify and report on compliance automatically and maintain continuous adherence to industry standards. By continuously reviewing and assessing cloud environment settings and configurations, we help you mitigate risks and ensure that your cloud infrastructure remains compliant, no matter how rapidly it evolves or how complex your deployments become.


What truly sets Automate Security apart is our commitment to full-stack risk analysis and intelligent prioritization. We go beyond mere detection, focusing on the risks that matter most to your business. Our platform provides deeply prioritized findings, leveraging advanced analytics to identify the most critical vulnerabilities and misconfigurations. This intelligent prioritization empowers Security and DevSecOps teams to automate threat detection and remediation efforts at scale. We enable you to mitigate and remediate risky privileges and faulty configurations through auto-generated and customizable policies, seamlessly integrating with your existing ticketing systems, CI/CD pipelines, Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools, and other essential workflows, ensuring a cohesive security posture.
